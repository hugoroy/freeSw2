Free Software

Free Software enables users’ rights.

What is Free Software?

“Free Software” means software that respects users' freedom and community. With Free Software, users of technology can have the power to use software freely, they can share copies of the software, they can study how it works to learn from it and to improve it so that software fits their needs.

Learn More

Why Free Software matters

Without Free Software, users cannot be in control of technology. Technology can be shaped and deployed to subjugate users: to control them, to spy on their actions and to disobey their commands.

Without Free Software, the technology that surrounds our society is out of control. Free Software contributes to a free and democratic society.
